Scallion Pancakes

Description

Scallion pancakes are a savory food often found in street food stalls as well as restaurants. The ingredients are simple but the result is a delicious flaky and crispy pancake. These pancakes can be frozen to be enjoyed at a later date.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup of hot water
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 4-5 stalks of green onions
  • Sea salt flakes
  • Vegetable or Canola oil

Steps

  1. Chop green onions
  2. Mix flour and kosher salt
  3. Add hot water to flour mixture slowly while mixing to create the dough
  4. Knead the dough for 5-8 min. or until smooth
  5. Let the dough rest for 30 min.
  6. Divide the dough into 3 pieces
  7. Roll the dough into a thin layer and lightly coat with oil and sprinkle green onions and sea salt flakes
  8. Roll dough into a snail shape and let rest for another 10 min.
  9. Roll dough into a flat pancake again
  10. Add oil into a pan and pan fry each pancake on both sides until crispy
