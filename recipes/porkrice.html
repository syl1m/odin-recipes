Taiwanese Braised Pork over Rice

Description

Braised Pork over rice is another popular Taiwanese dish. This dish uses braised pork belly that is cut into small pieces or even minced. The pork belly is braised for an hour or more to create its signature tender texture.

This is a traditional homestyle Taiwanese comfort food that goes great over rice or noodles. My family often makes this in large batches that will last for a couple days. It's a great leftover food and reheats well.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 lb pork belly
  • 2 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 Tbsp rock sugar
  • 2 cups of water
  • 6 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp rice wine
  • 3 Tbsp fried shallots
  • 1/2 tsp five spice powder
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Cut pork belly into small pieces
  2. Brown the pork belly and add the rock sugar so it caramelizes with the pork
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook until it starts to brown
  4. Add the remainder of the ingredients for the braising liquid
  5. Bring to a boil then simmer on Low heat for an hour
  6. Serve over a bowl of rice
